Information

ClassicPress is a WordPress 4.9 fork including the Classic Editor for an intuitive and easy editing experience. It is perfect for creators wanting to manage their sites on their own.

ClassicPress is completely customizable and extendable by means of plugins and themes to build every kind of website.
